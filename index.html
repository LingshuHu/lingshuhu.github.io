Hi there, I'm Lingshu Hu!

I'm a PhD Candidate at the Missouri School of Journalism and hold a graduate certificate in AI and Machine Learning. My research focuses on computational methods, media analytics, social media, and digital public. I was a data journalist and editor at the Guangzhou Daily, China. Read more about me...
